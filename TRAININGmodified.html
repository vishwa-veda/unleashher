Welcome to Unleash her Defense.

At Unleash her Defense, we provide comprehensive self-defense training tailored specifically for women. Our mission is to empower women by teaching practical skills to help protect themselves in any situation. Join our community of strong, confident women and take control of your safety!

Why Self-Defense?

In today's world, safety is a top priority. Our self-defense classes teach you how to:

  • Recognize and avoid dangerous situations.
  • Use practical techniques to defend yourself effectively.
  • Build confidence in your ability to protect yourself.
  • Increase your situational awareness.

Self-Defense Tactics

Groin Kick

A groin kick is a self-defense technique that can temporarily disable an attacker and make it easier to escape..

Heel-palm-strike

Palm heel strike may be a very effective tool in self defense, even more so than closed first technique.

Arm Grab

The arm grab is used to try and control or intimidate you.

Hair Grab

A hair grab technique is a self-defense move that can be used to counter an attacker who grabs your hair.

Alternate Elbow Strike

A powerful technique that can knock someone down or out if it lands cleanly.
