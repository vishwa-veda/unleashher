At Unleash her defense, we are committed to empowering women by providing them with essential self-defense skills and confidence to navigate the world with safety and strength. Our mission is to educate, equip, and inspire women to protect themselves in any situation by offering comprehensive, practical self-defense training.

Women are at risk of facing dangerous situations in the streets, at work or in public transportation…also within the ‘safety’ of their homes…and this is true of all countries whether developed or undeveloped, rich or poor, industrial or agrarian!

Our Vision

We envision a world where every woman not only feels secure but is empowered to act confidently in any situation. Our goal is to transform fear into strength by teaching women the physical and mental tools they need to protect themselves. By raising awareness and fostering a supportive community, we strive to create a society where self-defense is seen not just as a necessity but as a form of empowerment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a world where women can live without fear. We aim to provide women with the knowledge and skills necessary to defend themselves, mentally and physically, in any situation.By fostering self-confidence and raising awareness about personal safety, we strive to reduce the instances of violence against women, empowering them to take control of their own security and well-being.

Women’s self-defense is the practice of empowering women through training in techniques that help protect themselves from potential harm. Our organization offers classes and resources tailored to the needs and challenges women face.

Self-defense is vital for women because it equips them with the knowledge and skills to protect themselves in dangerous situations. It fosters confidence and independence, and teaches women how to respond to threats in their everyday lives.

We offer a variety of self-defense training programs, including basic self-defense techniques, situational awareness.We’ll show you the simple self defence moves you need to protect yourself - and explain how to avoid becoming a target in the first place..

Our Self-Defense Women course is designed for women and girls aged above 13.We encourage anyone who wants to feel safer, more confident, and better prepared to handle challenging situations to join us.

No prior experience is necessary. We provide beginner-friendly classes where instructors will guide you step-by-step, ensuring that you feel comfortable and confident as you learn the techniques.

You can sign up for a class through our website. Visit the "Help" section, choose the program you're interested in, and complete the registration process online. We also offer in-person sign-ups at our training centers.
